Fun facts about Birmingham!

  • Birmingham is the second most populated city in the United Kingdom, with one of the most densely populated places in the country barring London.
  • The anchor of the Titanic was made in the Black Country and over 50 festivals are celebrated across the city every year and it has Europe’s largest urban nature reserve!
  • It houses the UK’s busiest theatre with over 520,000 visitors each year and in 1875, three quarters of all pens in the world had nibs made in Birmingham.
  • From Birmingham you can reach 90% of the rest of the UK within 4 hours, and the city has around 34 million visitors a year.
